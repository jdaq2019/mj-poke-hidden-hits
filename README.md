# M&J Poke Hidden Hits

Welcome to **M&J Poke Hidden Hits**, an online marketplace dedicated to Pokémon card collectors and enthusiasts.

Our mission is to provide trainers and collectors with access to quality Pokémon cards, graded collectibles, sealed products, and exciting mystery packs featuring hidden chase cards.

## Features

* Pokémon Singles
* Graded Cards
* Mystery Packs
* Sealed Products
* Mobile-Friendly Design
* Responsive User Interface
* Secure Checkout (Planned)
* Inventory Management (Planned)

## Website Goals

M&J Poke Hidden Hits is designed to create a simple and enjoyable shopping experience for Pokémon collectors while offering opportunities to discover valuable cards through curated mystery packs and featured inventory.
